A little About Me

I'm a Australian Graphic Designer with a minor in Interior Design currently living in Ottawa, Ontario. Graphic design is not just something I do. My motto for my work is 'long story short' because design for me is a story or message that is simplified in a visual master piece.I believe that design can be much more than just something the looks good, It is in short world changing mind boggling, and emotive piece of art that has powerful impact on people.

Branding

My absolute favorite part of design, to be able to create or recreate an Identity for a company or business is hands down something I love doing and want to persue further.

Print and Packaging

Print is Also close to my heart because it is innovative and most importantly impactful

User Interface and WEB

The digital world is becoming more important every year, and it is a gigantic marketing tool for absolutely anybody.
